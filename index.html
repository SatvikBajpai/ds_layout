Operational Assumptions

Single-Person Picking

Aisle widths optimized for individual picker operation with standard equipment.

8-Hour Operation

Layout optimized for continuous operation with standard work shifts.

Single Loading Dock

One primary loading/unloading point for inventory replenishment.

Single Main Entrance

Primary entry point for staff and order fulfillment operations.

Industry Standards

Dark Store Best Practices

Based on industry leaders like Amazon, Instacart, and Ocado fulfillment centers. Optimized for high-velocity picking operations.

Safety Compliance

Performance Benchmarks

Layout efficiency targets based on top-performing grocery fulfillment operations (60-75% space utilization).

Constraints & Limitations

Grid-Based Placement

Racks are placed on a regular grid system for simplicity. Does not consider diagonal or custom orientations.

Static Load Distribution

Assumes uniform weight distribution. Does not account for floor load capacity variations.

Rectangular Layouts

Optimized for rectangular store footprints. Complex shapes may require manual adjustment.

Standard Equipment

Assumes standard picking carts and equipment. Specialized machinery may require wider aisles.
